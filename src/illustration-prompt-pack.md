# Illustration Prompt Pack

This pack is for generating future local sketch images outside the app. Do not call image APIs from the site. Place approved exports in `public/illustrations/final/` using the filenames below, then mark the matching asset as `final` in `src/data/illustrationAssets.ts`.

## Global Style

- Refined black graphite pencil sketch with light ink accents.
- White xuan paper / rice-paper background with subtle visible fibers.
- Eastern design studio concept drawing, architectural study, product concept sheet.
- Detailed but restrained: quiet linework, measured hatching, careful negative space.
- No real photos, no photorealism, no cartoon, no anime, no color overload.
- No text in image, no logo, no watermark.
- No people unless explicitly requested for a later asset.
