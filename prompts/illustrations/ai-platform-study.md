## English Prompt

Refined graphite pencil and subtle ink wash architectural concept sketch of an AI-assisted discovery platform for an Eastern aquatic design studio, abstract interface cards, matching paths, product and consultation preparation nodes, a small water vessel study embedded in the diagram, clean white xuan paper background with visible fine fibers, quiet technical restraint, no readable text, no logo, no watermark.

## Chinese Prompt

精致石墨铅笔与淡墨建筑式概念图，东方水景设计工作室的 AI 辅助发现平台，抽象界面卡片、匹配路径、产品与咨询准备节点，图中嵌入小型水器研究，洁净白色宣纸背景与可见细微纸纤维，技术感安静克制，无可读文字、无标志、无水印。

## Negative Prompt

No cartoon, no anime, no photorealism, no SaaS marketing dashboard, no glowing blue interface, no magic effect, no data-center imagery, no readable text, no logo, no watermark, no people.

## Style Notes

- Use abstract marks for interface elements, never readable labels.
- Keep the technology feeling quiet and architectural.
- Include one small aquatic object reference so it remains brand-specific.

## Cropping Notes

- Keep the diagram centered with generous margins.
- Avoid tiny UI details that become clutter.
- Make the asset readable in landscape panels and consultation discovery surfaces.

## Where It Appears In Site
