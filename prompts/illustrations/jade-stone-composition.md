Refined black graphite pencil and subtle ink wash sketch of jade-toned stones arranged for a calm Eastern aquascape composition, faceted mineral surfaces, shallow water reflection, sparse plant stems, architectural object-study layout, clean white xuan paper background with visible fibers, restrained material hatching, premium design studio mood, realistic stone forms, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Style Notes

- Stone should feel tactile and believable without looking like a jewelry ad.
- Keep tonal contrast soft and premium.
- Use negative space around the composition.

## Cropping Notes

- Keep the primary stone group within the central 70 percent.
- Allow top and bottom margin for portrait placement.
- Make the composition still readable in product cards.
