## Style Notes

- Keep the image bright, airy, and premium.
- Linework should feel like a product concept sketch and architectural object study.
- Rice-paper fiber should be visible but clean.
- Use graphite and very subtle ink wash only.

## Cropping Notes

- Keep the main vessel and waterline inside the central 75 percent.
- Leave enough quiet margin for hero and story panels.
- Avoid details that only work at large desktop size.
