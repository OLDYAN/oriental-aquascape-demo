# Aquatic Plant Study

## Filename

`aquatic-plant-study.webp`

## Target Path

`public/illustrations/final/aquatic-plant-study.webp`

## Aspect Ratio

4:5 export, square-card center safe.

## Recommended Size

1200x1500 px.

## English Prompt

Restrained graphite pencil botanical concept study for aquatic plants in a living water composition, submerged and emergent stems, soft roots, shallow waterline, minimal ceramic edge, Eastern design studio sketch, clean white xuan paper background with subtle visible fibers, precise line weight, quiet hatching, realistic plant structure, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Chinese Prompt

克制的石墨铅笔水生植物概念研究，活水构成中的沉水与挺水茎叶、柔和根系、浅水线与少量陶瓷器缘，东方设计工作室速写，洁净白色宣纸背景与细微可见纸纤维，线条精准，排线安静，植物结构真实，无文字、无标志、无水印。

## Negative Prompt

No cartoon, no anime, no photorealism, no stock illustration, no tropical excess, no bright flat green fill, no botanical textbook labels, no wallpaper pattern, no dirty paper, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Style Notes

- Keep the plants delicate, calm, and believable.
- Avoid lush jungle density.
- Preserve clean white paper around the plant forms.

## Cropping Notes

- Keep root and stem structure inside the central 70 percent.
- Make the silhouette readable in compact cards.
- Avoid fine details that become visual noise at small size.

## Where It Appears In Site

- Aquatic plant product card and drawer.
- Commerce preview.
- Category index for aquatic plants.
- Consultation builder when plant-focused direction is selected.
