## English Prompt

Refined graphite pencil and subtle ink wash studio sketch of ceramic vessels, a shallow water bowl, and jade-toned decorative pieces arranged with quiet negative space, Eastern aquatic interior design study, clean white rice-paper background with visible fibers, careful contour lines, restrained material hatching, realistic object forms, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Chinese Prompt

精致石墨铅笔与淡墨工作室速写，陶瓷器皿、浅水钵与玉色陈设小品，以安静留白组织，东方水景室内设计研究，洁净白色宣纸背景与可见纸纤维，轮廓线细腻，材料排线克制，器物形态真实，无文字、无标志、无水印。

## Negative Prompt

No cartoon, no anime, no photorealism, no stock still life, no antique shop clutter, no tourist souvenir styling, no red-and-gold ornament, no glossy product render, no text, no logo, no watermark.

## Style Notes

- The image should feel like a curated design studio object study.
- Keep object count low and placement calm.
- Avoid nostalgic antique-shop atmosphere.

## Cropping Notes

- Allow safe margins for square cards and shop panels.
- Do not put essential objects near the corners.
